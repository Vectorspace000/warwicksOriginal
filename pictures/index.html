GALLERIES

Hovering over the 'Galleries' tab at the top right of the page will give you access to the 'Re-enactors Gallery' and the 'Historical Gallery'. These pages will give you an idea of our group and its character and also allow you to see the real men of the British Army over 70 years ago. Our aim as a re-enactment group is to pay respect to these men through our portrayal and it's accuracy, but also having great fun at events and educating others about the history of the impression.
